我們的目標

Our Mission

Foster Mandarin Learning Globally

Cultivating supportive and immersive environments for effective Mandarin learning through our certified network of schools.

Promote Cultural Exchange

Creating meaningful connections between Mandarin learners and the vibrant local Taiwanese community around the world.

Encourage Community Participation

Fostering active participation through local events that promote cross-cultural understanding and practical language skills.

TCML

An official program by the Overseas Community Affairs Council, R.O.C. (Taiwan), dedicated to promoting Mandarin learning worldwide.
